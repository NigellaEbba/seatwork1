Pomeranians
The Pomeranian is a small dog (weighing no more than seven pounds) with a strong big dog temperament. Nearly two dozen colors, as well as other patterns and markings, are available for the thick double coat with its frill reaching over the chest and shoulders, although orange or red are the most prevalent choices.
Chow Chow
-Chows are large, powerful canines that can reach a shoulder height of 20 inches. Their lion's mane ruff around the head and shoulders is one of their unique characteristics.

Golden Retriever
-He is tThe Golden Retriever is a strong, muscular, medium-sized dog that gets its name from its dense, shiny coat of gold.
Dalmatian
-One of the most unusual coats in the animal kingdom is covered by the lovely, eye-catching spots of black or liver that characterize the Dalmatian. Under the spots is a trotting dog with delicate, exquisite proportions, measuring between 19 and 23 inches at the shoulder.
